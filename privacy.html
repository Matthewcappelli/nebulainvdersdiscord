Nebula Invaders

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

Overview

Nebula Invaders is a browser-based arcade game. This policy explains what information the game collects, how it is used, and how to contact us.

Information We Collect

The game stores your local best score in your browser using local storage. This information stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

If the game is launched as a Discord Activity, Discord may provide basic launch context to the activity iframe. This version of Nebula Invaders does not request Discord OAuth permissions, access your Discord account data, or store Discord user data.

How We Use Information

Local score data is used only to show your best score in the game. We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising.

Third-Party Services

The game may be hosted by a platform such as Railway and may run inside Discord. Those services may process technical information according to their own policies.

Data Retention

Your best score remains in your browser until you clear your browser storage. We do not maintain a server-side database for this game.

Children

Nebula Invaders is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been collected, contact us so it can be addressed.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game changes. The effective date above will be updated when meaningful changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the Discord application listing or GitHub repository.
